WCTU Rally in Town

Description: Rally tonight at 7PM at the town hall. 3 associates are speaking: @carry_nation @frances_willard @nealdow. End the saloon culture.

This poster appeals to activists of the Temperance movement because it captures the causes of family men getting into alcohol. The poster title "Temperance Builds Families" captures the importance of husbands quitting alcohol in order to have a strong bond with their family. The right side of the poster demonstrates the effect that alcohol has on families. With a father passed out on the table and a worried child crying upon his mother. This is contrast with the other side of the poster where a sober father is bonded with his child on a bright sunny day.
